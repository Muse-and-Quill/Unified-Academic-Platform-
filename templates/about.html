About Unified Academic Platform

The Unified Academic Platform (UAP) is a modern, all-in-one platform designed to streamline academic processes for students, faculty, and administrative teams. From course management to authentication and dashboard analytics, UAP focuses on clarity, speed, and beautiful design—built for institutions of all sizes.

Our Mission

To empower educational institutions with a fast, secure, and elegant platform that reduces manual work, enhances transparency, and creates a seamless experience for every student and staff member.

What We Value

  • Simple, clean, and responsive UI/UX
  • Privacy-focused login with email OTP
  • Automation-first academic workflows
  • Scalable architecture for any campus size
  • Secure student data handling

Built by the UAP Team

For collaborations, partnerships, or custom deployment, reach us via the Contact page.

Who Can Use It

Students, faculty, department admins, examination staff, and campus coordinators can all use the portal to manage tasks and stay updated in real time.

Core Features

OTP login, analytics dashboard, student records, course and attendance management, billing, notifications, and reporting—all in a single integrated platform.

Technology Stack

HTML & modern CSS on the frontend, Flask on the backend, and MongoDB on the database layer, making UAP flexible and scalable.
